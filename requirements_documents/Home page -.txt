4) Get To Know Us - 
  Domain Powers is one of India’s emerging leaders in the field of power factor correction and energy management, specializing in LT and HT APFC Panels engineered for industrial reliability and performance. With a commitment to technical precision and long-term energy efficiency, Domain Powers has established itself as a trusted name among electrical panel manufacturers and suppliers across India.

Our expertise lies in designing and manufacturing Automatic Power Factor Correction (APFC) Panels for both low-tension (LT) and high-tension (HT) applications. Each system is built using microprocessor-based intelligent relays, self-healing capacitor banks, and advanced protection circuits that ensure accurate reactive power compensation and stable power quality under all load conditions.

Driven by innovation and backed by deep industry knowledge, Domain Powers offers custom-engineered APFC solutions designed to maintain unity power factor, reduce demand charges, eliminate utility penalties, and enhance transformer capacity utilization. Our panels are developed in accordance with CPRI-approved designs and ISO 9001:2015 quality standards, ensuring the highest level of safety, reliability, and durability in operation.

At Domain Powers, every APFC panel reflects our focus on energy conservation, sustainable operation, and smart automation. Whether it’s an LT distribution network or an 11kV HT system, our panels integrate real-time monitoring, intelligent step switching, and harmonic-safe designs to deliver consistent results for industrial and commercial installations alike.

With a strong emphasis on power quality improvement, reactive power management, and energy-efficient control, Domain Powers continues to drive forward India’s electrical infrastructure. Our mission is to empower industries with precision-engineered LT and HT APFC panels that optimize performance, ensure compliance with utility norms, and contribute to long-term operational savings.

Recognized for our engineering excellence, customer-first approach, and dependable after-sales support, Domain Powers stands as a reliable partner for industries seeking high-performance APFC panels, capacitor banks, and power factor correction systems tailored to their specific needs.
